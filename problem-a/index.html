Simon

Simon (no last name) is the main character of the anime Gurren Lagann which is also known as Tengen Toppa. He starts off as an innocent curious child in the anime series, but quickly becomes a great and honored leader. He goes through many challenges throughout the series which he perseveres. Most notably, the death of his beloved father/brother figure Kamina which ends up having a lasting impact on Simon. The character development he goes through in the span of only 27 episodes of this anime makes it truly a worthwhile watch.

I admire:

  • His persistance and endurance; like many anime characters he never gives up and pushes through.
  • His style because it's on point!
  • The bond he has with his friends and family is inspiring.
